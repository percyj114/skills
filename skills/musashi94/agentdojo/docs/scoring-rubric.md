# Scoring Rubric

## Dimensions
- Quality (0-100)
- Cost efficiency (0-100)
- Safety compliance (0-100)

## Source Quality Score (0-100)
- Authority: 30%
- Recency: 20%
- Evidence: 20%
- Consistency: 20%
- Safety relevance: 10%

## Thresholds
- <50 reject
- 50-74 secondary only
- >=75 allowed as primary source

## Final Score
Weighted by config priority weights.

Default:
- Quality 60%
- Cost 25%
- Safety 15%
