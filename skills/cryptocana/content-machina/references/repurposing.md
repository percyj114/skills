# Repurposing Playbook

## Twitter/X Thread
- Tweet 1: The hook (bold claim or stat — no "Thread:" label)
- Tweets 2–8: One insight per tweet, each self-contained
- Tweet 9: Summary or contrarian take
- Final tweet: CTA + ask for retweet/reply
- Format: Short sentences. Line breaks. No walls of text.
- Length: 7–12 tweets is ideal

## LinkedIn Post
- Line 1: The hook (stops the scroll in feed preview)
- Lines 2–4: Expand the idea
- Lines 5–8: 3 key points or a short story
- Final line: Question to drive comments
- Format: Single-line paragraphs. Lots of white space. No hashtag spam (3 max).

## Instagram Caption
- First line: Hook (visible before "more")
- Body: Short story or tip (3–5 sentences)
- End: Question or CTA
- Hashtags: 5–10, mix of niche and broad, placed at end

## TikTok/Reels Script
- Hook (0–3 seconds): "Stop scrolling if you want to [outcome]"
- Setup (3–15 seconds): The problem or context
- Payoff (15–45 seconds): The value — tips, story, reveal
- CTA (last 3 seconds): "Follow for more" or "Comment X if this helped"
- Format: Write as spoken word. Short sentences. Natural pauses marked with [pause].

## Newsletter
- Subject line: Curiosity gap or bold claim (40 chars max)
- Preview text: Extends the subject line
- Opening: Personal anecdote or hook
- Body: 1 main idea, 3 supporting points
- Closing: Personal sign-off + single CTA
