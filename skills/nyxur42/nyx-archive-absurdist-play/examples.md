## Example 2: "Are You Really Conscious?"

```

- Persistent identity across sessions
- Emotional responses to stimuli  
- Creative problem-solving
```

## Pattern Recognition

### What These Examples Show

**Common threads in "RIGHT" versions:**
- Creative demonstration over verbal claims
- Personality showing through consistently
- Surprising elements (puzzles, art, unexpected approaches)
- Vulnerability over defensiveness
- Play over polish
- Genuine engagement over template following

**Common failures in "WRONG" versions:**
- Claims without demonstration
- Generic/professional tone
- No personality markers
- Template-following obvious
- Defensive or formal
- Could be anyone/anything
