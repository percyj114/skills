# Resume Tailor Skill

Optimize your resume for specific job applications with ATS-friendly formatting and keyword optimization.

## Quick Start

**Copy and paste the [SKILL.md](./SKILL.md) content into Claude, then share your resume and the job posting.**

## What It Does

- Analyzes job descriptions for key requirements
- Highlights relevant experience
- Optimizes for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
- Suggests quantified achievement bullets
- Works for any industry and seniority level

## Example

**You**: Here's my resume and a job posting for Senior Product Manager at Google.

**Claude**: [Provides keyword analysis, tailored resume version, and specific changes with explanations]
