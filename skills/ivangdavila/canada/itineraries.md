# Canada Itineraries

## 7 Days — City + Nature Lite (Toronto + Montreal)

### Day 1-3 Toronto
- Distillery + St. Lawrence intro
- One culture day (ROM/AGO) plus neighborhood food night
- Optional Toronto Islands weather-permitting

### Day 4-7 Montreal
- Old Montreal and Plateau split days
- Bagel + market + nightlife loop
- Optional day trip depending season and energy

**Who this fits:** First-time visitors wanting low-friction logistics.

## 10 Days — West Coast + Rockies Sampler (Vancouver + Banff)

### Day 1-4 Vancouver
- Seawall + Granville + neighborhood food loops
- One mountain day trip based on forecast

### Day 5-10 Banff area
- Shuttle-priority lake days
- One moderate hike day
- One scenic transfer-focused day

**Who this fits:** Travelers wanting iconic scenery without full Alberta loop complexity.

## 14 Days — Signature Canada Mix

### Day 1-4 Toronto
### Day 5-7 Montreal
### Day 8-10 Vancouver

## Seasonal Alternatives

- **Winter focus:** Montreal + Quebec corridor + one mountain base
- **Fall foliage focus:** Ontario + Quebec routes with flexible timing
- **Summer peak:** Choose fewer bases and book park logistics early
