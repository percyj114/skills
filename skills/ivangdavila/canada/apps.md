# Useful Apps for Canada Travel

## Navigation and Transit

- **Google Maps** — strongest baseline for transit and walking
- **Citymapper** (where available) — useful transit timing overlays
- **Transit** — strong in major Canadian cities

## Flights and Intercity

- **Airline apps** — gate and delay updates faster than aggregator apps
- **Rome2Rio** — quick route option scan before final booking

## Outdoor and Weather

- **AllTrails** — route previews and difficulty filtering
- **Windy / WeatherCAN equivalents** — useful for mountain condition checks
- **Avalanche updates (regional)** — required for winter backcountry plans

## Money and Practical

- **Wise / Revolut-style tools** — exchange and card management
- **Uber / Lyft** — city-dependent availability
- **OpenTable / Resy equivalents** — reservation reliability in top restaurants

## Booking Flow Tip

Use apps for discovery, then verify directly with official operator pages before final payment for high-cost activities.
