## Emergency Numbers

- **Police / Fire / Ambulance:** 911
- **Non-emergency medical advice:** province-dependent hotlines

## Health and Medical

- Travel insurance is highly recommended for visitors.
- Urgent care and ER wait times vary by city and season.
- Keep medication list and key documents accessible offline.

## Outdoor Safety

- Check weather and trail conditions before departure.
- Respect wildlife distance rules.
- Carry water, layers, and backup power.
- Tell someone your route for longer hikes.

## Urban Safety Basics

- Standard city awareness applies in nightlife and transit hubs.
- Use licensed transport at late hours.
- Keep backup payment method in case of card issues.

## Winter Risk Notes

- Ice and low visibility increase driving and walking risk.
- Keep extra transit time buffer.
- Wear proper footwear, not just insulated sneakers.

## When to Escalate

- Call 911 for immediate danger, medical emergencies, or active incidents.
- For non-urgent issues, use local non-emergency channels and official advisories.
