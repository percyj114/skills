## Dress Code Guide

| Venue Type | Dress Code | Examples |
|------------|------------|----------|
| **Michelin 2-3 star** | Smart elegant, closed shoes | FZN, STAY, Trèsind Studio |
| **Upscale restaurants** | Smart casual (collared shirt, no shorts) | Nobu, Zuma, Coya |
| **Hotel restaurants** | Smart casual minimum | Most hotel dining |
| **Casual licensed** | Relaxed smart (avoid flip-flops) | Bistro des Arts, JB's |
| **Unlicensed independents** | Casual acceptable | 3Fils, Orfali Bros |

**Men:** Long trousers, collared shirt, closed shoes for fine dining. No shorts, sandals, or athletic wear.  
**Women:** More flexibility. Elegant dress or smart separates. Avoid revealing outfits.

## Booking Tips

**Hard to book (1-2+ months ahead):**
- FZN
- Trèsind Studio
- 3Fils
- Zuma (especially weekends)

**Book 1-2 weeks ahead:**
- Nobu
- STAY
- Ossiano
- Most celebrity chef spots

**Walk-in usually OK:**
- Bread Street Kitchen
- Jamie Oliver's
- Most casual dining
