## Cross-Category Traits

When recommending, consider:

| Trait | Apps that match |
|-------|-----------------|
| Privacy-focused | Signal, ProtonMail, Bitwarden |
| Offline-first | Bear, iA Writer, Strong |
| No subscription | One-time purchase options |
| Cross-platform | Works on iOS + Android + Web |
| Apple ecosystem | Deep integration with iCloud, Shortcuts |
