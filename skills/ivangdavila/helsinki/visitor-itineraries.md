# Helsinki Itineraries

## 1 Day - Highlights

### Morning (9:00-12:00)
1. **Senate Square and Cathedral** - Start at the iconic landmark
2. **Old Market Hall** - Coffee and pastry, browse local foods
3. **Market Square** - Waterfront atmosphere, fresh produce

### Lunch (12:00-13:30)
- **Story** at Old Market Hall (modern Finnish)
- Or grab salmon soup from market stall

### Afternoon (13:30-17:00)
4. **Ferry to Suomenlinna** - 15 min ride from Market Square
5. **Explore fortress** - Museums, ramparts, tunnels
6. **Return ferry** - Aim for 16:00 back

### Evening (17:00-21:00)
7. **Walk Esplanadi** - Central park and shops
8. **Dinner in Punavuori** - Design District restaurants
9. **Optional**: Allas Sea Pool sauna (closes 21:00)

### 1 Day Alternatives

**Design Focus**: Design District, Oodi Library, Kiasma Museum
**Local Life**: Kallio brunch, flea market, public sauna, local bar

## 3 Days - Comprehensive

### Day 1: City Center
- Morning: Senate Square, Cathedral, Market Square
- Lunch: Old Market Hall
- Afternoon: Suomenlinna (half day)
- Evening: Dinner in Kruununhaka or Katajanokka

### Day 2: Design and Culture
- Morning: Oodi Library, explore freely
- Late morning: Design District shopping/galleries
- Lunch: Punavuori cafe
- Afternoon: Temppeliaukio Church, Kiasma Museum
- Evening: Kallio neighborhood, dinner and bars

### Day 3: Local Experience
- Morning: Hakaniemi Market Hall, browse and brunch
- Midday: Public sauna (Allas or Loyly)
- Afternoon: Choose one:
  - Nuuksio National Park (nature)
  - Porvoo day trip (old town)
  - Museum day (Ateneum, National Museum)
- Evening: Final dinner, recommendations from locals

## 7 Days - Deep Dive

### Day 1-3: As above

### Day 4: Greater Helsinki
- Morning: Espoo Tapiola (planned garden city)
- Visit: EMMA Art Museum or Nuuksio National Park
- Evening: Dinner in Lauttasaari

- 7:00 Ferry to Tallinn (2h)

- Full day Nuuksio National Park
- Hiking, swimming, picnic
- Or: Archipelago tour if summer

### Day 7: Wrap Up
- Morning: Any missed attractions
- Shopping: Stockmann, Design District, souvenirs
- Final public sauna experience
- Farewell dinner

## Seasonal Adjustments

### Summer (Jun-Aug)
- Add: Island hopping, outdoor swimming, terrace dining
- Extend: Longer evenings, everything stays open late
- Consider: Music festivals, white nights experience

### Winter (Dec-Feb)
- Add: Christmas markets (Dec), Lux Helsinki (Jan)
- Adjust: More indoor activities, museums, saunas
- Morning start: Later (daylight 9:00-15:00)
- Essential: Warm layers, daylight optimization

### Shoulder Seasons
- Spring (Apr-May): Vappu celebrations (May 1), melting season
- Fall (Sep-Oct): Ruska colors, mushroom picking season

## Practical Tips

### Walking Distances
- Market Square to Senate Square: 5 min
- Senate Square to Design District: 15 min
- Central Station to Kallio: 20 min
- Most attractions walkable from center

### HSL Day Tickets
- 1 day AB zone: EUR 9.50
- 3 days AB zone: EUR 19.00
- Covers metro, trams, buses, ferries (including Suomenlinna)

### Restaurant Reservations
- Weekends: Book popular places 2-3 days ahead
- Weekdays: Usually walk-in OK
- Fine dining: Book week ahead

### Museum Tips
- Most closed Mondays
- Free entry often first Friday of month
- Helsinki Card includes many museums
