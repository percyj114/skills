# Utrecht — Itineraries

## 1 Day in Utrecht

### Essential Utrecht (Full Day)

**Morning (9:00-13:00)**
1. Start at Utrecht Centraal, grab coffee at Station café
2. Walk to Dom Tower (book 10:00 slot in advance)
3. Climb Dom Tower — views of entire city and beyond
4. Explore Pandhof cloister garden (peaceful, often empty)
5. Walk through Domkerk (free, beautiful interior)

**Lunch (13:00-14:30)**
- Option A: Terrace on Oudegracht — Café Ledig Erf or Restaurant Gys
- Option B: Grab broodje (sandwich) and sit by canal

**Afternoon (14:30-18:00)**
1. Walk along Oudegracht — best canal section
2. Museum Speelklok (worth the 1.5 hours, very unique)
3. Explore side streets: Drieharingstraat, Zadelstraat
4. Coffee break at Blackbird Coffee or Juice Plus

**Evening (18:00-21:00)**
1. Aperitivo at Olivier (former church, gorgeous interior)
2. Dinner reservation at Blauw (Indonesian rijsttafel) or De Rechtbank
3. Post-dinner walk: Trajectum Lumen light route

## 2 Days in Utrecht

### Day 1: Classic Utrecht
Follow 1-day itinerary above.

### Day 2: Deep Dive

**Morning (10:00-13:00)**
1. Centraal Museum (Rietveld, Dick Bruna, Utrecht masters)
2. If traveling with kids: Miffy Museum instead
3. Walk through Williamspark area — posh neighborhood

**Lunch (13:00-14:30)**
- Broodje Ben (near museum, excellent sandwiches)
- Or picnic in park if weather is good

**Afternoon (14:30-18:00)**
1. Rietveld Schröder House (pre-book required, UNESCO site)
2. Take tram back to center
3. Explore Lombok neighborhood — multicultural, great food
4. Browse shops on Voorstraat (independent stores)

**Evening (18:00-22:00)**
1. Dinner in Lombok: Eetcafé Buurten or Middle Eastern options
2. Drinks at Café België (300+ beers) or Drogisterij
3. Live music at TivoliVredenburg (check program)

## 3 Days in Utrecht

### Day 1 & 2
Follow 2-day itinerary.

### Day 3: Beyond the Center

**Option A: Railway Museum Day**
- Spoorwegmuseum (full day, 4+ hours)
- Great for families and train enthusiasts
- Lunch on site
- Evening: drinks in center

**Option B: Day Trip**
Pick one:
- **Amsterdam** (25 min): Rijksmuseum, Anne Frank House, canals
- **Rotterdam** (40 min): Markthal, Cube Houses, architecture
- **Gouda** (20 min, Thu morning Apr-Aug): Cheese market
- **Kinderdijk** (1h): UNESCO windmills

**Option C: Active Day**
- Morning: Rent bike, cycle to Amelisweerd forest
- Picnic lunch
- Afternoon: Return via Bunnik, explore eastern Utrecht
- Evening: Deservedly lazy dinner on terrace

## 5 Days in Utrecht

### Day 1-3
Follow 3-day itinerary.

### Day 4: University & Science

**Morning**
1. Utrecht Science Park (Uithof campus)
2. University Museum (science, medicine history)
3. Botanical Gardens (two locations — Fort Hoofddijk is impressive)

**Lunch**
- Campus options or cycle back to Lombok

**Afternoon**
1. Explore Oost neighborhood (student-friendly, relaxed)
2. DOMunder (archaeological site beneath Dom Square)
3. Shopping: Twijnstraat, vintage and design shops

**Evening**
- Concert at TivoliVredenburg or
- Local brewery tour (De Leckere or Maximus)

### Day 5: Choose Your Adventure

**Culture Day**
- Morning: 's-Hertogenbosch day trip (Bosch art, stunning cathedral)
- Return via Amersfoort (quick stop)

**Active Day**
- Cycle to Wijk bij Duurstede (30km, beautiful route)
- Historic fortress town
- Ferry crossing included
- Return by train

**Relaxation Day**
- Sleep in
- Coffee at specialty cafe
- Long lunch at restaurant with Michelin recognition
- Spa time (Spa Bliss or similar)
- Shopping for Dutch design

## 7 Days in Utrecht

- Mauritshuis (Vermeer's Girl with a Pearl Earring)

## Tips for Any Itinerary

### Book in Advance
- Dom Tower (popular, limited slots)
- Rietveld Schröder House (required, small groups)
- TivoliVredenburg (sells out for popular acts)
- Dinner at popular restaurants (weekends)

### Skip If Short on Time
- Large canal boat tours (walk instead)
- Generic shopping streets
- Long museum visits if not interested

### Don't Underestimate
- Walking distances (compact but hilly in parts)
- Coffee/terrace time (essential Dutch experience)
- Weather changes (always have layers)

### Enhance Your Visit
- Download NS app for trains
- Get OV-chipkaart for easy transit
- Rent bike if staying 2+ days
- Check festival calendar — something always happening
